
# Store System - Complete User Guide

Welcome to the Retail Management System. This guide will walk you through every part of the application, explaining how each feature connects to the next. By following this guide, you will be able to manage inventory, process sales, and track financial performance seamlessly.

---

## 1. Getting Started: The Dashboard
**What it is:** The control center of your shop.
**How it works:**
As soon as you log in, you are greeted by the **Dashboard**. This page gives you an instant snapshot of your business health. You don't need to calculate anything; the system does it for you.
- **Top Cards:** See your total sales regarding Items count, total income (money in), and total expenses (money out) at a glance.
- **Recent Activity:** A quick list of the latest actions taken in the store.

---

## 2. Setting Up Your Shop (The Foundation)
Before you can sell anything, you need to tell the system what you carry. This happens in three simple steps.

### Step A: Categories & Brands
**Where:** The *Category* and *Brand* pages.
**Why:** To keep your inventory organized.
- **Categories:** Create broad groups like "Smartphones", "Accessories", or "Cables".
- **Brands:** Add the manufacturers you sell, like "Apple", "Samsung", or "Anker".
*Tip: You must create these first because every item belongs to a specific Category and Brand.*

### Step B: Defining Items
**Where:** The *Items* page.
**How:** Click "Add Item".
**The Critical Distinction:**
The system is smart enough to handle two types of products. You must choose the right one:
1.  **Standard Items (Accessories):** Things like cases or chargers. You only care about *how many* you have.
2.  **Devices (IMEI Tracked):** Expensive electronics like Phones or Tablets. For these, the system needs to know *exactly which specific unit* is being sold.
    - *Action:* When creating a phone, check the "Is Device?" box. This tells the system to ask for a unique Serial Number (IMEI) for every single unit you buy.

---

## 3. Stock Management (Adding Inventory)
**Where:** The *Items* page.
**Scenario:** A new shipment of goods has arrived.
**How to Process:**
1.  Find the item in your list.
2.  Click the **"Add Stock"** (or Purchase) button.
3.  **For Standard Items:** Simply type the quantity (e.g., "50") and the cost price. The potential stock number updates instantly.
4.  **For Devices (Phones):** The system prevents mistakes. It will ask you to scan or type the **IMEI** for *each* phone.
    - *Why?* This ensures you never sell a phone you don't actually have, and prevents selling the same phone twice.
    
---

## 4. Point of Sale (Making a Sale/POS)
**Where:** The *POS* or *Sales* page.
**The Flow:**
This is where all your setup pays off. The selling process is designed to be fast and error-proof.

1.  **Building the Cart:**
    - Search for a product by name or filter by brand.
    - Click "Add to Cart".
2.  **The "Device" Safety Check:**
    - If you add a generic item (like a cable), it just goes into the cart.
    - **However, if you add a Phone:** A popup will appear asking, *"Which specific phone are you selling?"* You will see a list of available IMEIs in stock. You must select one.
    - *Result:* This guarantees your inventory records are 100% accurate down to the specific unit.
3.  **Checkout:**
    - Review the total price.
    - Click "Complete Sale".
4.  **The Receipt:**
    - Instantly after the sale, a professional **PDF Receipt** is generated. You can print this for the customer or save it.

---

## 5. After the Sale (Tracking & History)
**Where:** The *Sales History* page.
**What happens:**
Once a sale is complete, the item is permanently removed from your sellable stock.
- You can view a full list of every transaction ever made.
- You can search specifically by customer name or date to find old records.
- If you sold a Device, that specific IMEI is marked as "Sold" in the database so it can never be sold again by accident.

---

## Summary of the Flow
1.  **Create** Organization (Brands/Categories).
2.  **Define** Products (and mark valuable ones as "Devices").
3.  **Buy** Stock (scanning IMEIs for devices).
4.  **Sell** in POS (selecting the specific IMEI).
5.  **Print** Receipt.
